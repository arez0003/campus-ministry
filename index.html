OUR LEADERS


Campus Ministry at Algonquin College has been bringing a Christian dimension to college life since 1994. We seek to strengthen and support the Christian community on campus while respecting and engaging people of other faiths and world views.

Campus Ministry counsellors focus on spiritual and faith issues such as purpose of life, self worth, relationships, values, guilt, death, life crises, God and religion.
