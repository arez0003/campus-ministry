VOLUNTEER

"As each has received a gift, use it to serve one another, as good stewards of God's varied grace" (1 Peter 4:10)

There are many ways to volunteer with Campus Ministry and to be recognized by Algonquin College’s Co-Curricular Program.

Administrative Assistant

Assist the Campus Minister by handling administrative tasks within the Campus Ministry Office.

Greeter

Campus Ministry Greeters welcome visitors to the E211 Spiritual Centre and provide hospitality, information and referrals.

Pastoral Minister

In the context of the Christian faith, Pastoral Volunteers, as members of the CMAC Pastoral Care team, are present with, listen to and extend a helping hand to any one in the Algonquin College community who is hurting, confused, lonely or simply curious about life’s large and small questions.

Represent Campus Ministry throughout the College






Help with meals, organize social events






Be a good steward and join weekly clean ups of campus grounds
