WHO WE ARE

"For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand, that we should walk in them. Ephesians 2:10


Campus Ministry welcomes all students and staff. We offer Christian counselling, prayer, bible study and fellowship. We have been bringing a Christian dimension to college life since 1994.

Campus Ministry seeks to strengthen and support the Christian community on campus while respecting and engaging people of other faiths and world views.



How do Campus Ministry and College Counselling Differ?

College counsellors focus on education, career and personal issues. Campus Ministry counsellors focus on spiritual and faith issues such as purpose of life, self worth, relationships, values, guilt, death, life crises, God and religion.
