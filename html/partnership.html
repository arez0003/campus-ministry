Partnering with Campus Ministry at Algonquin College

There are opportunities for Christian organizations desiring to do ministry at Algonquin College to do so be it for a day, a week, or a semester. Campus Ministry has (or has had) partnerships with various Christian denominations for a range of activities from Bible studies and Alpha programs to lunch-time discussions and Eucharist services. The Campus Minister can provide support in terms of networking and advice on venues and publicity. Students have appreciated the contribution of our Christian partners, so we very much welcome any interest in the type of ministry or programming your talents and resources that your organization may wish to offer. All partnerships are reviewed by the Campus Ministry Board of Directors, and all our partners are provided with an orientation including the relevant college policies that govern our conduct and responsibilities towards the students we aim to minister to.

If you wish more information including the partnership agreement, please contact the Campus Minister at derkacy@algonquincollege.com or Jack Wilson at wilsonjohns@rogers.com
